ESCAPE OR DIE TRYING

  • Play as Subject 17, a tortured test subject infused with uncontrollable rage
  • Brutal combat meets psychological horror in a deranged asylum
  • Core Mechanic: The Urge Meter - kill to survive or succumb to madness
  • Linear narrative with escalating horror and revelations

GAME DESIGN PILLARS

VISERAL COMBAT

Physics-driven gore system with brutal melee and ranged options. Every hit feels impactful and devastating.

URGENCY MECHANICS

The Urge Meter forces constant aggression - hesitate and you'll self-destruct from built-up rage.

PSYCHOLOGICAL HORROR

A twisted narrative of experimentation and revenge, told through environmental storytelling.

CORE GAMEPLAY LOOP

Engage in brutal combat

→

Kill enemies to reset Urge Meter

→

Progress through asylum

→

Repeat or self-destruct

Health-by-kill system rewards aggression while punishing hesitation

THE URGE METER

  • Constantly fills over time - creates relentless tension
  • Resets with each kill - rewarding aggressive play
  • At 100%: Screen distorts, controls invert, health drains
  • Final stage: Instant self-destruction if not reset

Current Urge Meter: 75% - Danger Zone

ASYLUM LEVELS

  • Cell Block: Starting area - learn basic mechanics
  • Morgue: First major horror set pieces
  • Maintenance Wing: Environmental puzzles introduced
  • Observation Halls: Story revelations
  • The Core Lab: Final confrontation

Each level introduces new enemies, weapons, and story elements
